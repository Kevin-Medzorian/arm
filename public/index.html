Digital Receipts with Elegance.





Why digitize receipts?

Physical receipts are bad for the environment.

Physical receipts are a product of the past, and most end up going to waste. They also pollute our environment and negatively impact the Earth's climate due to CO2 emissions.





Physical reciepts facilitate the spread of disease.

Physical receipts act as surface that can transmit diseases from the cashier to the customer. This process is eliminated by using digital receipts.
